## Overview

Iquea Commerce provides an intuitive shopping experience with featured collections, category browsing, and powerful search capabilities. This guide walks you through discovering and viewing products.

## Browsing the Product Catalog

The product catalog is accessible from the main navigation and displays all available furniture items.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Product Catalog">
    Navigate to **Products** from the main menu or click **"Ver todos los productos"** from the homepage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Product Grid">
    Products are displayed in a grid layout showing:
    - Product image
    - Product name
    - Category label
    - Price in euros (€)
    - Star rating
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Product Count">
    The total number of available products is displayed at the top of the catalog: **"X productos"**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Featured Products Section

The homepage showcases a curated **Featured Collection** ("Colección Destacada") with up to 6 highlighted items.

### What Makes Products Featured?

- Featured products are marked with `es_destacado: true` in the system
- The API endpoint `/api/productos/destacados` returns these special items
- Featured items may display promotional badges (e.g., "-30%")

<Note>
Featured products represent hand-selected pieces that define contemporary elegance and are crafted with quality materials and timeless design.
</Note>

### Featured Section Details

The featured section includes:
- **Hero Image**: Large banner with the new 2025 collection
- **Product Cards**: Visual grid of 6 selected products
- **Ratings**: Star ratings (4-5 stars) displayed for each item
- **Quick Access**: Direct links to product detail pages

## Searching and Filtering Products

### Search Functionality

You can search for products by name using the search feature:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Search Query">
    Type your search term in the search bar (e.g., "Sofá", "Mesa", "Lámpara")
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Results">
    The page header will show: **"Resultados para '[your query]'"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clear Search">
    Remove the search query to return to the full catalog
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filter by Category

Narrow down products using the category sidebar:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Category Filter">
    View the **"Categorías"** section in the left sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Category">
    Click on any category button:
    - **Todas** - Show all products
    - **Salón** - Living room furniture
    - **Comedor** - Dining room furniture
    - **Dormitorio** - Bedroom furniture
    - **Iluminación** - Lighting
    - **Mesas** - Tables
  </Step>

  <Step title="Active Category Highlight">
    The selected category button will be highlighted as **active**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filter by Price Range

Refine your search by setting minimum and maximum price limits:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Price Filter">
    Find the **"Precio (€)"** section in the left sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Minimum Price">
    Enter your minimum price in the **Min** input field
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Maximum Price">
    Enter your maximum price in the **Max** input field (default: 10000€)
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Filtered Results">
    Products automatically update to show only items within your price range
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Filters work together - you can combine category and price filters to narrow your search further.
</Note>

### Clear All Filters

If no products match your criteria:

1. A message appears: **"No se encontraron productos que coincidan con tu búsqueda"**
2. Click **"Limpiar filtros"** to reset all filters
3. This clears category selection, price range, and search query

## Viewing Product Details

Click on any product card to view comprehensive product information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Product Card">
    Select any product from the catalog or featured section
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Product Information">
    The detail page displays:
    - Large product image
    - Product name and category
    - Current price with currency
    - Full product description
    - SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
    - Dimensions (height × width × depth in cm)
    - Stock availability (number of units)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Stock Status">
    Product availability is clearly indicated:
    - **"X unidades"** - Available stock count
    - **"Agotado"** - Out of stock (shown in red)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Product Detail Components

#### Product Metadata

Each product page shows structured information:

- **SKU**: Unique product identifier
- **Dimensiones**: Physical dimensions for furniture sizing
- **Stock**: Real-time inventory count

#### Product Images

High-quality product images are displayed prominently. If no image is available, a placeholder is shown.

#### Pricing Information

Prices are displayed with:
- Large, clear price amount
- Currency symbol (€ for euros)
- Formatted with two decimal places

<Warning>
You must be logged in to add products to your cart. Guest users will see a prompt to **"Iniciar Sesión para Comprar"**.
</Warning>
